Campus Carnival

  • December 18, 2024 10:59 am
  • Shareyer Shawon

Here’s a full content description for the Campus Carnival event: Campus Carnival: A Day of Fun, Food, and Friendship! Join us for the annual Campus Carnival, a day packed with excitement, entertainment, and community spirit! This event is the perfect way to take a break from studying, meet new people, and enjoy a variety of fun activities.

Event Highlights

Games & Rides: Test your skills and enjoy thrilling rides! From classic carnival games to inflatable obstacle courses, there’s something for everyone. Live Performances: Enjoy live music and performances by student bands and dance groups, creating a vibrant atmosphere throughout the day. Food Stalls: Satisfy your taste buds with a wide range of delicious foods, including local favorites, international cuisine, and sweet treats. Photo Booths: Capture the memories with friends at our themed photo booths. Perfect for fun snapshots!.
